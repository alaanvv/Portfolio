About Me

Hi there! My name is Alan, and I'm a full stack developer. At 17 years old, I'm passionate about creating high-quality, creative and minimalist software solutions that can make a real difference in people's lives.

If you're interested in learning more about me and my work, feel free to check my social accounts. I'm always excited to connect with other developers and collaborate on new projects, so don't hesitate to get in touch!
